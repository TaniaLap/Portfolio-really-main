About me

Front-end developer from Mykolaiv, Ukraine

When I received a diploma in government and administration, I went to work in my specialty, but it was very boring, I realized that I needed something creative. Then I started learning Photoshop. I immersed myself in it completely, studied Lightroom, Illustrator and now I am an experienced designer who has been working for 7 years in an advertising firm. But at this stage, I again realized that this was not enough and I needed to move on, then my friend advised me to study at SheCodes, but I laughed very loudly, because I was 100% sure that coding was not for me, I thought that it's so complicated that my brain can't handle it, but I took a chance. And now I am incredibly happy that I am getting this knowledge. I immersed myself in the codes completely as I once did in Photoshop, which means that I will not stop, I have room to strive and grow.

Now I am a certified Front-end Developer and my journey is just started!

I'm currently seeking new opportunities in front-end development. I'm open to work remotely or on-site and have flexible hours. Some of the tools I'm skilled at are: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VSCode, Bootstrap, API, GitHub, with some background experience in SEO.
